# Tango

Dancer, bandoneón player, and milonga organizer based in Tokyo and Kyoto.

Performing since 2015, collaborating internationally with partner Erik.

- Bandoneón — KANSAI Tango Orquesta and collaborative projects

- Organizer — Kyoto Tango Club (weekly, since 2018)

- Awards — 1st Place, Tango Pista Asia Championship West Japan (2019)
