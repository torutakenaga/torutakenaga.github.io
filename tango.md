# Tango

Dancer, bandoneón player, and milonga organizer based in Tokyo and Kyoto.
Performing since 2015, collaborating internationally with partner Erik.

---

## Highlights

- **Performer** — Shows, milongas, and concerts across Japan and internationally (Seoul, online)
- **Bandoneón** — Performing with KANSAI Tango Orquesta and collaborative projects
- **Organizer** — Kyoto Tango Club (weekly, since 2018), international artist tours
- **Awards** — 1st Place, Tango Pista Asia Championship West Japan (2019); Winner, OPEN DUTCH TANGUERIL (2021)
- **Media** — FM Kyoto Radio, ABC TV appearances
